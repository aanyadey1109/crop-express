CONSERVATION RESOURCES

RESOURCES

General Information on Endangered Crops

Crop Trust
https://www.croptrust.org
Global Crop Diversity Trust, focuses on preserving crop diversity worldwide.

FAO - Plant Genetic Resources
http://www.fao.org/plant-genetic-resources/en
Information on the global state of crop genetic resources and conservation strategies.

Slow Food Foundation for Biodiversity
https://www.fondazioneslowfood.com
Dedicated to preserving agricultural biodiversity, including endangered crops.

National Plant Germplasm System (USDA)
https://www.ars-grin.gov
Offers germplasm and information about crop conservation.

Farming Techniques and Conservation

International Federation of Organic Agriculture Movements (IFOAM)
https://www.ifoam.bio
Resources on sustainable and organic farming practices.

Conservation Agriculture Network (CAN)
https://www.fao.org/agriculture
Techniques on soil conservation and sustainable agriculture.

Rodale Institute
https://www.rodaleinstitute.org
Focuses on regenerative organic agriculture.

Bioversity International
https://www.bioversityinternational.org
Research and data on agricultural biodiversity and conservation.

General Biodiversity Conservation

Seed Savers Exchange
https://www.seedsavers.org
Connects people to save and share heirloom seeds.

Global Seed Vault (Svalbard)
https://www.seedvault.no
Ensures backup storage for global crop diversity.

International Seed Federation (ISF)
https://www.worldseed.org
Works to facilitate global seed trade and preserve crop diversity.

Contacts for Further Engagement

World Agroforestry Centre (ICRAF)
https://www.worldagroforestry.org
Contacts for agroforestry-based conservation projects.

Contact a Local Agricultural University
Universities like Wageningen (Netherlands), UC Davis (USA), and ICRISAT (India) often have specialized departments working on crop conservation.

Global Forum on Agricultural Research and Innovation (GFAR)
https://www.gfar.net
Connects stakeholders in agriculture and conservation.
